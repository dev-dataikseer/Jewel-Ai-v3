  CATALOG_IMAGE / VIRTUAL_TRY_ON → fal-ai/nano-banana-pro/edit

  GEMSTONE / BACKGROUND → fal-ai/flux-2-max/edit

  CUSTOM → openai/gpt-image-2/edit
