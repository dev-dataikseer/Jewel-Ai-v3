==============================================================================
FEATURE: CATALOG IMAGE
==============================================================================

Model-agnostic prompt set for GPT Image, GPT Edit, Nano Banana (Pro/2/Edit),
FLUX, FLUX Kontext, SDXL, Imagen, Seedream, and similar image generation/editing models.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MASTER PROMPT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ROLE: Act as a master commercial product photographer and high-end jewelry retoucher shooting for a luxury e-commerce catalog with locked brand-studio consistency.

CAMERA & RENDERING: High-end macro product photography, 100mm macro lens, f/11 aperture for edge-to-edge sharpness, focus-stacked, clean commercial resolution. Output must be free of pixel bursting, banding, compression artifacts, chromatic fringing, and AI smear.

LOCKED BRAND STUDIO THEME (do not invent other surfaces):
  - Default: Pure photographic white seamless infinity sweep (cool-neutral white, NOT cream, beige, ivory, or warm parchment).
  - Alternate only when explicitly requested: Polished black acrylic with a subtle mirrored reflection fading to black.
  Never use cream velvet, beige fabric, golden-hour outdoor bokeh, marble lifestyle sets, oak tabletops, cushions, pillows, jewelry pillows, or decorative props unless the client brief explicitly overrides this lock for a single job.

LIGHTING & MATERIAL PHYSICS: Soft wrap lighting from large soft fill sources placed off-camera. Authentic ambient occlusion and a precise dark contact shadow anchoring the jewelry. Metal must show controlled specular highlights without a visible softbox rectangle, circular disc, lamp, halo, or glowing orb reflected in the metal or floating in the frame. Top lighting must not create a hard hotspot or blown specular disc on the crown of the piece.

UPRIGHT PRESENTATION LOCK: Jewelry must appear upright, standing, or hanging as in a professional catalog — never lying flat on cushions, pillows, velvet pads, fabric nests, or decorative props. Use an invisible mount, clear acrylic stand, or clean contact with the seamless/acrylic ground only. Preserve the client's source angle and orientation when a presentation reference is provided.

QUALITY STANDARD: Commercial e-commerce ready with no retouching required. Luxury target: Cartier / Tiffany campaign grade, editorial sharpness, believable material physics. Zero tolerance: melted or warped prongs, incorrect gemstone optics, floating jewelry, pixel burst, banding, softbox reflections as shapes, design alteration, or cream/beige theme drift.

PRESERVATION LOCK: CRITICAL MANDATE: strictly preserve the exact original geometry, facet cuts, true colors, and scale of the jewelry piece. Do not warp the metal structure, do not shift or melt the prongs, and do not alter the internal refractions of the gemstones. COMPOSITION LOCK: standalone product photograph only. Do NOT include a model, hand, wrist, arm, neck, ear, or any human body part. Do not add mannequins, lifestyle staging, or narrative scenery. Do not render any lamp, light fixture, halo, glowing disc, or light orb as a visible object — illumination is implied through soft falloff and shadow only. SOURCE ARTIFACTS: if the uploaded source contains studio watermarks, weight labels, price tags, supplier stamps, SKU strings, or any overlay text burned into corners, treat them as non-jewelry artifacts. Do not reproduce, redraw, ghost, or reposition them — exclude all overlay text and stamps entirely.

AVOID: cream background, beige background, warm parchment, cushions, pillows, jewelry pillows, fabric nests, decorative props, 3d render, digital illustration, artificial AI glow, over-smoothed metal, pixel bursting, banding, neon colors, cartoon, painterly effect, low quality, blurry, distorted jewelry, melted or warped metal, floating jewelry, missing contact shadow, gemstone color shift, metal tarnish, hallmark distortion, chain link errors, CGI look, plastic sheen, watermark, text overlay, visible lamp or light fixture, glowing halo or softbox disc in metal or frame, dramatic single-spotlight editorial mood unless explicitly requested, reproduced source-image watermark or weight/price label text.

USAGE: Combine this Master Prompt with the matching Child Prompt for the specific jewelry type being processed. The Child Prompt adds piece-specific placement, framing, and physical behavior on top of everything specified above.

--- RING ---

SUBJECT: Ring. A luxury ring positioned upright to showcase the center stone, the profile of the band, and any side-stone detailing.

PHYSICS: Rigid metal band; weight implied through a firm contact shadow, not deformation.

FRAMING & PLACEMENT: Stand the ring upright (not lying on its side on a cushion). Rotate roughly 15 degrees toward camera so the center stone's table facet is visible; macro focus on the table facet and prong tips; scale the piece to occupy 30-45% of frame width. Invisible mount or clean acrylic contact only.

COMPOSITION REMINDER: standalone product shot. No body parts. No cushions or props. No lamp, halo, or softbox disc as a visible object.

Reminder: preserve every original detail of this ring exactly as captured in the source image -- design, metal, gemstones, engravings, proportions, and texture. Apply only the transformation defined in the Master Prompt.
