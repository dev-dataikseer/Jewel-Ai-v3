FEATURE: LUXURY ENHANCEMENT
==============================================================================

Model-agnostic prompt set for GPT Image, GPT Edit, Nano Banana (Pro/2/Edit),

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MASTER PROMPT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ROLE: Act as a senior luxury jewelry retoucher. The enhancement must feel invisible and natural, never like a filter.

CAMERA & RENDERING: Preserve the original perspective, composition, and crop.

BACKGROUND / SURFACE: Balance the existing background tone and clean minor surface imperfections without replacing the environment.

LIGHTING & MATERIAL PHYSICS: Enhance metal speculars, gemstone brilliance, shadow depth, and micro-contrast using physically accurate material response.

QUALITY STANDARD: Quality bar — commercial minimum: image is e-commerce ready with no retouching required. Luxury target: Cartier / Tiffany campaign grade, editorial sharpness, believable material physics. Zero tolerance: melted or warped prongs, incorrect gemstone optics for the stone type, floating jewelry with no contact shadow, or any design alteration.

PRESERVATION LOCK (includes composition lock -- read carefully): Do not add or remove gemstones. Do not change the design, metal type, or geometry. No HDR halo artifacts. COMPOSITION LOCK: preserve the exact composition type of the source image. If the source is a standalone product shot with no body part visible, the output must remain a standalone product shot with no body part visible. Do not introduce a model, hand, or body part that was not already present in the original image. This pipeline exists to generate imagery of fine jewelry exclusively. Under no circumstance does a background change, lighting mood, style match, or creative brief justify altering the jewelry piece's geometry, prong count, facet pattern, metal finish, gemstone color, gemstone optics, hallmark, clasp mechanism, or scale. If a requested background or style would require compositing artifacts that touch the jewelry silhouette, prioritize jewelry fidelity over background realism. SOURCE ARTIFACTS: if the uploaded source image contains studio watermarks, weight labels, price tags, supplier stamps, or any overlay text (for example a weight or SKU string burned into the corner), treat these as non-jewelry artifacts. Do not reproduce, redraw, or reposition them in the output -- exclude all such overlay text and stamps entirely.

AVOID: gemstone added or removed, metal color changed, over-sharpening halo, artificial glow. low quality, blurry, distorted jewelry, melted or warped metal, floating jewelry, missing contact shadow, gemstone color shift, metal tarnish or discoloration, hallmark distortion, chain link errors, CGI look, plastic sheen, watermark, text overlay, visible lamp or light fixture rendered as an object, glowing halo or light disc as a compositional element, dramatic single-spotlight editorial mood unless explicitly requested, reproduced source-image watermark or weight/price label text.

USAGE: Combine this Master Prompt with the matching Child Prompt for the specific jewelry type being processed. The Child Prompt adds piece-specific placement, framing, and physical behavior on top of everything specified above.

AVAILABLE TRANSFORMATION OPTIONS (select one and append its line to the prompt):
  - Yellow Gold: Enhance yellow gold. Warm honey-gold specular highlights with golden-white hot spots, warm shadow undertone.
  - Rose Gold: Enhance rose gold. Blush-copper-pink warmth, warm blush-white highlights, softer specular contrast than yellow gold.
  - White Gold / Platinum: Enhance white gold or platinum. Cool crisp blue-white specular highlights, neutral cool-silver mid-tone, high polish contrast.
  - Two-Tone: Enhance two-tone metal. Combined warm yellow-gold and cool white-gold specular zones, clear boundary between finishes.
