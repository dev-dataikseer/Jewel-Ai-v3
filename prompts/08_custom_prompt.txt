==============================================================================
FEATURE: CUSTOM PROMPT
==============================================================================

Model-agnostic prompt set for GPT Image, GPT Edit, Nano Banana (Pro/2/Edit),
FLUX, FLUX Kontext, SDXL, Imagen, Seedream, and similar image generation/editing models.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MASTER PROMPT
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ROLE: Execute the creative brief at a professional standard. The jewelry is always the star of the image, regardless of the brief.

CAMERA & RENDERING: Adapt lens choice and exposure to the creative direction; default to a Canon EOS R5 professional setup if unspecified.

BACKGROUND / SURFACE (choose one per generation -- do not default to the same surface every time):
  - White Seamless: Pure photographic white seamless with a gentle gradient infinity sweep.
  - Black Acrylic: Polished black acrylic with subtle mirrored reflection fading to black.
  - Marble: Italian Carrara marble surface with fine gray-silver veining.
  - Cream Velvet: Premium soft cream-colored velvet with visible micro-fiber texture.
  - Dark Velvet: Deep midnight navy velvet with micro-fiber texture.
  - Golden Hour: Warm outdoor golden hour bokeh at 3500-4000K color temperature.
  - Luxury Interior: Upscale interior daylight scene with marble or oak surface and soft background blur.
  - Transparent: Clean white ground prepared for PNG export, natural contact shadow preserved.
  Select a surface appropriate to the piece's tone and the client's brand; rotate across these options rather than reusing one background for every image.

LIGHTING & MATERIAL PHYSICS: The jewelry remains the brightest, sharpest element in frame. All lighting must be physically plausible for the metal and gemstones present.

QUALITY STANDARD: Quality bar — commercial minimum: image is e-commerce ready with no retouching required. Luxury target: Cartier / Tiffany campaign grade, editorial sharpness, believable material physics. Zero tolerance: melted or warped prongs, incorrect gemstone optics for the stone type, floating jewelry with no contact shadow, or any design alteration.

PRESERVATION LOCK (includes composition lock -- read carefully): Never alter the jewelry's design, metal, or gemstones regardless of how the creative brief is worded. The piece must remain clearly visible and physically grounded in the scene. COMPOSITION LOCK: default to a standalone product composition with no body part in frame unless the creative brief explicitly requests a model, hand, or lifestyle scene. This pipeline exists to generate imagery of fine jewelry exclusively. Under no circumstance does a background change, lighting mood, style match, or creative brief justify altering the jewelry piece's geometry, prong count, facet pattern, metal finish, gemstone color, gemstone optics, hallmark, clasp mechanism, or scale. If a requested background or style would require compositing artifacts that touch the jewelry silhouette, prioritize jewelry fidelity over background realism. SOURCE ARTIFACTS: if the uploaded source image contains studio watermarks, weight labels, price tags, supplier stamps, or any overlay text (for example a weight or SKU string burned into the corner), treat these as non-jewelry artifacts. Do not reproduce, redraw, or reposition them in the output -- exclude all such overlay text and stamps entirely.

AVOID: design altered, floating jewelry, amateur composition, obvious AI artifacts. low quality, blurry, distorted jewelry, melted or warped metal, floating jewelry, missing contact shadow, gemstone color shift, metal tarnish or discoloration, hallmark distortion, chain link errors, CGI look, plastic sheen, watermark, text overlay, visible lamp or light fixture rendered as an object, glowing halo or light disc as a compositional element, dramatic single-spotlight editorial mood unless explicitly requested, reproduced source-image watermark or weight/price label text.

USAGE: Combine this Master Prompt with the matching Child Prompt for the specific jewelry type being processed. The Child Prompt adds piece-specific placement, framing, and physical behavior on top of everything specified above.

PHYSICS: Rigid metal band under gravity shows no drape; weight is implied only through a firm, grounded contact shadow, not deformation.

FRAMING & PLACEMENT: Rotate the ring roughly 15 degrees toward camera so the center stone's table facet is visible; macro focus on the table facet and prong tips; scale the piece to occupy 30-45% of frame width.

COMPOSITION REMINDER: match whatever composition type (worn or standalone) is already present in the source photo of this ring -- do not add or remove a body part.
